About Us

The Kungiyar Hadinkan Hausa Da Hausawan Duniya Foundation is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of the Hausa people while providing support to communities in need.

Our Mission

To empower Hausa communities through education, cultural preservation, and social support.

Our Vision

A world where Hausa culture thrives and communities are self-sufficient and resilient.
